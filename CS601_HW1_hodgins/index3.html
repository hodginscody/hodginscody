About my Band

I sing and play the guitar in a Top 40's band called 2TheTop. We have performed at various venues, including the Yankee Candle Village, Fourth of July celebrations, and private parties. My friend Sash' and I started the band back in 2015, and we've added members since. We write and produce original music as well as cover popular artists such as:

  • Ed Sheeran
  • Justin Timberlake
  • The Weeknd
  • Backstreet Boys
Feel free to listen to our original music on our Soundcloud page.
