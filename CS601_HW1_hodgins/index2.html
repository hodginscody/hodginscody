About our Business

Our flight simulator, AirWolf, will be FAA approved as an Advanced Aviation Training Device (AATD). An AATD allows pilots to count hours towards their ratings. An AATD is over half the cost per hour cheaper to rent than an actual aircraft, so pilots can save alot of money utilizing this. The following table compares how many hours pilots can log in an AATD for different ratings:

Rating Hours
Private Pilot 2.5
Instrument 20
Commercial 50
Airline Transport 25
